# Lesson One's Lesson Plan 第一课的课程计划

## Lesson Learning 课上学啥

Creating and responding to vocal sounds

发出嗓音，跟它互动

## Teaching activities 教学活动

### Chase the sound 跟踪响声

Play a game for exploring the pitch of a vocal sound.

玩游戏，探索声乐的音高。

Children:

- explore making different pitches and sounds with their voices as they respond to a hand action;

- understand pitch through the use of voice and movement.


孩子们：

- 跟随手势，用嘴发声，体会各种音色和音高的效果；

- 通过嗓音和运动，理解音高的概念。


### Start with singing 开始唱吧

Explore vocal sounds and add them to a song.

尝试用嗓子发音，然后加到歌声里

Children:

- look at pictures to stimulate ideas for exploring vocal sounds;

- differentiate between different lengths and volumes (dynamics) of vocal sounds; 

-  sing a song which includes a variety of vocal sounds.

孩子们：

-  探索嗓音的各种可能，看图来激发更多主意；

-  区分嗓音的不同长度和不同力度 (dynamics)；

- 唱歌，包含嗓音的多种变化。

### Playing at the park 在公园里玩

Add high, low and sliding vocal sounds to a song.

给歌加入高的、低的和滑行的嗓音

Children:

- use a variety of pitches to create vocal sounds that represent pictures of playground activities;

- sing a song, adding their own vocal sounds to the end of each verse.

孩子们：

- 游乐场里有各种活动，用变化的音高来表达图示场景；

- 唱歌，在每段歌词的末尾加入自己的嗓音。

## Vocabulary 词库

- Pitch：音高

- Dynamics：力度

## Extended Learning 拓展学习

Create a sound collage of a park. Different groups use their voices to make the sounds of different pieces of equipment. 

创作声音拼贴画，描绘公园场景。各组用自己的声音，表现不同的器材。

# Lesson 2: Glad to be me 第 2 课：我就是我

## Lesson Learning 课程主题

- Creating and responding to vocal sounds 

- Exploring how to change sounds

----

- 发出嗓音，作出反应

- 试着改变声音


## Teaching activities 教学活动

### Say it...说出来

Perform a poem that demonstrates different ways of using the voice

表演小诗，展示多种用嘴发声的方式

Children:

- learn a poem as they explore a range of expressive voices;

- use a variety of dimensions (pitch, tempo and dynamics) as they perform a poem expressively.

孩子们：

- 学首诗，同时尝试一系列富有表现力的发声方式；

- 通过音高、节奏和力度等多维变化，把小诗演得更有表情。

### Glad to be me 我就是我

Learn a happy song and add whole body actions

学首欢快的歌，用上全身动作

Children:

- learn a song and think about how to sing and perform the lyrics expressively;

_ make up actions that match the vocabulary of the song lyrics.

孩子们：

- 学首歌，同时想想，怎么才能把歌唱得、演得有感染力；

- 想出几个动作，配合歌词里的说法。

### Glad to be me performance 我就是我 - 表演

Choreograph an action song performance

设计一幕戏，演出这首歌

Children:

- perform a choreographed song with actions;

- perform the song in groups, joining in one group at a time to build a performance.

孩子们：

- 用动作表演一首精心编排的歌曲；

- 分组表演歌曲，一次加入一组，作出表演效果

## Vocabulary 词库

Pitch: 音高

Tempo：节奏

Dynamics：力度 

## Extended Learning 拓展学习

Listen to some compositions that use extended vocal techniques, eg Cathy Berberian (1925 1985) created a piece called Stripsody that only uses sounds found in comics, eg ‘splat’, ‘vroom’ and ‘brr’.

听这样的作品，它们只使用扩展的声乐技法。例如凯茜·柏柏尔人 (1925 1985) Cathy Berberian (1925 1985) 创作的名为 Stripsody 的作品，只使用漫画中发现的声音，例如 ‘splat’, ‘vroom’ 和 ‘brr’。

# Lesson 3: A surprise meeting 意外的相遇

## Lesson Learning 课堂学习

- Creating and placing vocal and body percussion sounds 

- Exploring descriptive sounds

----

- 发出声音，排布声音，声音来自人声和身体打击乐。

- 试试各种具有描述性的声音。

## Teaching Activities

### Show me one click 敲一下我看看

Perform a body percussion song

演奏一首有身体打击乐曲的歌

Children:

- learn a song and perform body percussion sounds that increase in number with each verse.

孩子们

- 学习一首歌曲，演奏身体打击乐，拍数随着一段一段的歌词而增加。

### A surprise meeting 意外的相遇

Add expressive vocal sounds and body percussion to a story

拿到一个故事，为它添加富有表现力的声音，添加身体打击乐。

Children:

- explore a variety of vocal and body percussion sounds to accompany a story;
- follow a story board score as they explore making their expressive sounds; 

- change the tempo and dynamics of sounds to make them more expressive.

孩子们

- 配合着一个故事，尝试各种人声，各种身体打击乐的声音来伴奏；

- 在他们探索发出富有表现力的声音时，遵循故事板乐谱

- 改变声音的节奏和力度，使其更具表现力。

### A surprise ending 意外的结局

Explore the expressive effect of a different ending to a story

探索故事不同结局的表演效果。

Children:

- suggest different endings for a story;

- explore sounds that can be used to accompany the suggested endings;

- perform the different endings with accompanying expressive sounds.

孩子们

- 建议故事的不同结局；

- 为所建议的结尾尝试不同的声响；

- 表演不同的结局，用上不同的声响。

## What you will need 你要用到

- A selection of hand-held percussion instruments

----

- 挑选出一些手持式的打击乐器

## Vocabulary 词库

- Pitch：音高

- Dynamics：力度 

- Tempo：节奏

## Extended Learning 扩展学习

Create your own story with vocal and body percussion accompaniment.

创造你自己的故事，用声乐和身体打击乐伴奏。
